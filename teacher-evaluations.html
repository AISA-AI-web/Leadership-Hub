Instruction

Guiding Question: How effectively am I engaging students, asking questions, differentiating, and using resources to support learning?

Engagement
ADEK 1.1, 3.1
Reflective Prompts
  • Did I circulate and check on all students during the lesson?
  • How many students answered questions today – was it the same few or the whole class?
  • Did I create moments of joy, energy, or challenge that kept students engaged?
Questioning
ADEK 1.3, 3.4
Reflective Prompts
  • Did my questions today push students to explain their thinking, not just give quick answers?
  • How often did I ask students to build off each other's ideas?
Differentiation
ADEK 2.1, 3.3
Reflective Prompts
  • Did I notice who needed extra support or challenge throughout the lesson?
  • How did I respond in the moment when a student didn't understand?
Use of Technology
ADEK 3.1
Reflective Prompts
  • Were the resources I used essential to student learning or just an add-on?
  • Did technology help students collaborate, explore, or show their understanding in a deeper way?
